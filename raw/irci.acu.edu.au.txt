                                                <a data-toggle="collapse" href="#collapse-4" data-parent="#content" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse-4" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="tab" data-track-label="Theology and&#160;Philosophy&#160;">
                                                    Theology and&#160;Philosophy&#160;
                                                </a>
                                            </h3>
                                        </div>
                                        <div id="collapse-4" class="collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="heading-4">
                                            <div class="card-body-container">
<div class='row common-padding'>                                                            <div class="col-md-6">
                                                                <h5>ACU Centre for Liturgy</h5>
                                                                <p>The ACU Centre for Liturgy provides specialist expertise, teaching, research and formation in liturgical studies, sacramental theology and the sacred arts. We offer formative education for parish ministers through to higher degree research students.</p>
                                                                    <a class="arrow-link" href="/about-acu/institutes-academies-and-centres/acu-centre-for-liturgy" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="call to action" data-track-label="Learn more">Learn more</a>
                                                            </div>
                                                            <div class="col-md-6">
                                                                <h5>Institute for Religion and Critical Inquiry</h5>
                                                                <p>The Institute for Religion and Critical Inquiry (IRCI) is a leading centre for the study of religion and theology in its historical and contemporary contexts. Our research comprises three collaborative and interdisciplinary programs: biblical and early Christian studies; medieval and early modern studies; and religion and theology.</p>
                                                                    <a class="arrow-link" href="/research-and-enterprise/our-research-institutes/institute-for-religion-and-critical-inquiry" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="call to action" data-track-label="Learn more">Learn more</a>
                                                            </div>
</div><div class='row common-padding'>                                                            <div class="col-md-6">
                                                                <h5>Research Centre for Studies of the Second Vatican Council</h5>
                                                                <p>The Research Centre for Studies of the Second Vatican Council advances understanding of the Second Vatican Council (1962&ndash;1965), considered the most significant event in the history of the Catholic Church in the 20th century. The Centre aims to investigate lines of inquiry that draw their inspiration from the Second Vatican Council, among them, biblical theology, comparative theology and ecological theology. </p>
                                                                    <a class="arrow-link" href="/research-and-enterprise/our-research-institutes/research-centre-for-studies-of-the-second-vatican-council" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="call to action" data-track-label="Learn more">Learn more</a>
                                                            </div>
</div>
                                            </div>
                                        </div>
                                    </div>
                                    <div id="pane5" class="card tab-pane fade  " role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="tab-5">
                                        <div class="card-header" role="tab" id="heading-5">
                                            <h3 class="mb-0">
                                                <a data-toggle="collapse" href="#collapse-5" data-parent="#content" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse-5" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="tab" data-track-label="Other">
                                                    Other
                                                </a>
                                            </h3>
                                        </div>
                                        <div id="collapse-5" class="collapse" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="heading-5">
                                            <div class="card-body-container">
<div class='row common-padding'>                                                            <div class="col-md-6">
                                                                <h5>Plunkett Centre for Ethics</h5>
                                                                <p>The Plunkett Centre for Ethics expresses its commitment to intellectual and professional excellence through research, teaching, consultation, and community engagement, and by bringing a Catholic perspective to all its endeavours. Its goals are to deepen and advance knowledge and understanding of the ethical standards which, relevant to the pursuit of human health and well-being, are found in clinical practice, medical research, business and management practice, and the allocation of healthcare resources.</p>
                                                                    <a class="arrow-link" href="/about-acu/institutes-academies-and-centres/plunkett-centre-for-ethics" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="call to action" data-track-label="Learn more">Learn more</a>
                                                            </div>
                                                            <div class="col-md-6">
                                                                <h5>Queensland Bioethics Centre</h5>
                                                                <p>The Queensland Bioethics Centre focuses on high-quality research, education, and consultancy in the area of bioethics, with engagement and impact locally, nationally and internationally.</p>
                                                                    <a class="arrow-link" href="/about-acu/institutes-academies-and-centres/queensland-bioethics-centre" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="call to action" data-track-label="Learn more">Learn more</a>
                                                            </div>
</div><div class='row common-padding'>                                                            <div class="col-md-6">
                                                                <h5>Veteran Life Research</h5>
                                                                <p>The Veteran Life Research team takes a multi-disciplinary approach to exploring the post-service life experiences of Australian veterans.
We undertake research focused on advancing an evidence-based understanding of the life challenges veterans can experience after they transition from service. Our ambition is to use this knowledge to inform policy and advance best-practice in the support of veterans both nationally and internationally.</p>
                                                                    <a class="arrow-link" href="/about-acu/leadership-and-governance/leadership/vice-chancellor-and-president/research/veteran-life-research" data-track-category="filter tabs" data-track-action="call to action" data-track-label="Learn more">Learn more</a>
                                                            </div>
