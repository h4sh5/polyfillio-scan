								<h1 class="h2">Who we are</h1><div class="wysiwyg"><h5>The Aged Care Workforce Remote Accord is made up of a group of employers and experts delivering aged care services in remote and very remote areas of Australia.</h5>
<p>We work collaboratively with services and communities across the nation to document the challenges facing the workforce and understand existing strategies aimed at combatting those challenges. We are running a Workforce Implementation Project, and building an evidence-based, provider-led advocacy platform. We engage with communities, key stakeholders, and Government to ensure an effective and informed approach to applying provider-led solutions.</p>
